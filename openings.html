Department of Computational and Data Sciences, Indian Institute of Science

Applications are invited for Project Assistant position for various Computer Vision projects involving use of Deep Learning. Candidates are expected to be available for at least 8 months full-time .

Minimum Qualifications:

  1. About to graduate / Graduated with B.Tech or M.Tech degrees in Electrical Engg./Electronics Engg./Computer Science
  2. Completed coursework involving Linear Algebra, Probability, Image Processing, Deep Learning and Machine Learning
  3. Proficient with python, C++, PyTorch, and TensorFlow. .


Candidates who have previously worked on advanced Computer Vision/Deep Learning projects will be given priority.

Project Assistants are expected to be quick learners, willing to take initiative in driving projects and expected to work towards paper submissions to top-tier conferences. After an initial probationary period, PAs will have the freedom to work on problems of interest with the approval of Prof. Venkatesh Babu.

Salary: A consolidated salary of Rs. 25,000/- to Rs. 45,000/- per month depending on  qualification and experience. 

Duration: Initially for 8 months duration and extendable further depending up on  performance.
