Convenor

Venkatesh Babu received his Ph.D degree from Dept. of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. Thereafter, he held postdoctoral positions at NTNU, Norway and IRISA/INRIA, Rennes, France, through ERCIM fellowship. Subsequently, he worked as a research fellow at NTU, Singapore. He spent a couple of years working in the industry before returning to the institute in August 2010. He is currently a Professor and Chair of the Dept. of Computational and Data Sciences (CDS), IISc, where he leads Vision and AI Lab (VAL) (formerly, Video Analytics Lab) at IISc. His research interests include Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Multimedia. He is a recipient of the SERB Star (2020) and Sathish Dhawan Young Engineer Awards (2019). He served as a Program Chair of the AIML-Systems 2023 conference and serving as General Chair of ICVGIP'24.
