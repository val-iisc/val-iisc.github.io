Prospective Students



We invite individuals with a research aptitude towards signal processing, image processing, pattern recognition and applied machine learning to be part of our research group

Please consult IISc admissions website regarding the eligibility and dates of admission. For our department (CDS) specific info, please visit this link. To join Video Analytics Lab (VAL) at CDS in Ph.D / M.Tech(Res) programs, you will have to get admitted to IISc first!. If you hold a M.E./M.Tech degree (you are directly eligible for IISc research interview, you do not require a GATE score) or hold a B.Tech./M.Sc. with a valid GATE score/CSIR-UGC-NET qualifications, do ensure you choose CDS as your departmental preference in IISc application.

Post-Docs: We are also looking for post-docs with strong publication records through IISc's Raman Post-Doc fellowship

In case you have any questions, please send an email to: venky @ iisc . ac . in
